## Welcome

Welcome to **Zelkea**, the universal money movement protocol designed to make moving money as simple as sending an email.

Instead of routing payments using complex and sensitive bank account numbers, credit cards information, or mobile wallets details, Zelkea introduces a unified financial identifier. Every participant on the network receives a simple, human-friendly financial handle (e.g., `malick@mpesa` or `tom@citi`). Zelkea safely routes payment requests across institutions globally using a single API, without sensitive financial details ever leaving the user's home bank.

## What you can build with Zelkea

- **Universal Checkouts**\
  Accept online and offline payments through a single implementation, regardless of what bank or wallet the customer uses.
- **Split Marketplace Payments**\
  Automatically split a single incoming checkout payment during execution to route funds to multiple parties simultaneously, bypassing complex secondary payout setups.
- **Instant Peer-to-Peer Transfers**\
  Route direct account-to-account transfers globally from any participating platform, bank or fintech platform instantly.
