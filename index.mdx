Welcome to Zelkea, the global financial network rebuilding the foundations of money movement.

Zelkea allows financial institutions to issue a human-readable handle, such as malick@wallet or tom@bank, to their customers so they can send, receive, and pay online. On the network, settlement happens in real time, cross-border transfers don't exist as a separate concept, and customer information never leaves the issuing organization.
