## Welcome

Welcome to **Zelkea**, the universal financial routing protocol designed to make moving money as simple as sending an email.

Instead of routing payments using complex and sensitive bank account numbers, credit card details, or mobile wallet credentials, Zelkea introduces a unified financial addressing layer. Every participant on the network receives a simple, human-friendly financial handle (e.g., `malick@mpesa` or `tom@citi`). Zelkea settles transactions instantly by moving funds directly between the balances of participating institutions and platforms.

## What you can build with Zelkea

- **Universal Checkouts**\
  Accept online and offline payments through a single implementation, regardless of the country, bank, or mobile wallet the customer uses.
- **Split Marketplace Payments**\
  Automatically divide a single checkout payment during execution to route funds to multiple parties instantly, completely bypassing complex secondary payout infrastructure.
- **Instant Peer-to-Peer Transfers**\
  Route direct account-to-account transfers globally between any participating banks or fintech platforms in seconds.
- **Cardless & POS-less Retail**\
  Enable physical merchants to accept instant payments using simple business handles, eliminating the need for expensive POS hardware terminals.
