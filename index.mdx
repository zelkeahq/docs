Welcome to **Zelkea**, the global financial network that makes moving money as simple as sending an email.

Instead of relying on long, sensitive bank account numbers or card details, Zelkea uses simple financial identities called handles. Every network participant receives a human-readable handle (such as `malick@wallet` or `tom@bank`) that can be used to send, receive, and make payments.

## What you can build with Zelkea

- **Universal checkouts**<br />Accept online payments through a single integration, regardless of what country, bank, or mobile wallet your customer uses.
- **Split marketplace payments**<br />Automatically divide a single payment at checkout to distribute funds to multiple vendors instantly, removing the need for complex payout setups.
- **Instant peer-to-peer transfers**<br />Instant account-to-account transfers globally between participating financial institutions.
- **Cardless retail payments**<br />Enable physical merchants to accept instant payments by sharing their handle, eliminating expensive card swipe fees and POS hardware.
