Welcome to **Zelkea**, the global payment network designed to make moving money as simple as sending an email.

Instead of routing payments using long, sensitive bank account numbers or credit card details, Zelkea uses simple financial identities. Every network participant receives a human-friendly handle (such as `malick@mpesa` or `tom@citi`) to send, receive, and pay instantly. Instead of waiting days for traditional settlement clearing, Zelkea instantly shifts funds directly between participating institutions.

## What you can power with Zelkea

- **Universal Checkouts**\
  Accept online and offline payments through a single integration, regardless of what country, bank, or mobile wallet your customer uses.
- **Split Marketplace Payments**\
  Automatically divide a single payment at checkout to distribute funds to multiple vendors instantly, removing the need for complex payout setups.
- **Instant Peer-to-Peer Transfers**\
  Route direct account-to-account transfers globally between different banks or fintech platforms in seconds.
- **Cardless Retail Payments**\
  Enable physical merchants to accept instant payments using simple business handles , eliminating expensive card swipe fees and POS hardware.
