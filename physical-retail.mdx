---
title: "Physical retail"
description: "Get paid by customers in person without a card reader, a POS terminal, or losing a percentage to card networks."
---

A physical retailer with a Zelkea handle can receive in-person payments without any additional hardware. No card reader, no POS terminal, and no monthly equipment fees. All they need is a Zelkea business account or an account at a participating financial institution.

## What the transaction looks like at the counter

The customer opens their banking or wallet app, enters the merchant's handle, such as `shop@ca`, and enters the amount. It works like sending money to a friend or family member. The payment is delivered instantly to the merchant's bank account or Zelkea account, regardless of which bank or wallet the customer uses.

## Why this matters for small businesses

Traditional card payments often come with transaction fees, hardware costs, and payout delays. With Zelkea, none of these apply:

- **No hardware** — Displaying or sharing your handle is all you need to receive payments.
- **No payout delays** — Funds are available as soon as the customer completes the payment, making it easier to pay suppliers and manage cash flow.
- **No card-related fraud risks** — There are no card numbers to steal, clone, or expose because the payment does not rely on cards.
- **Simple pricing** — A transfer through Zelkea costs \$0.10 regardless of the amount, paid by the customer. The merchant receives the full amount.

Because some financial institutions support USSD and phone banking, Zelkea transfers can also work without an internet connection. This makes it suitable for regions where connectivity may be limited.
