---
title: "Creator economy"
description: "Pay creators directly, wherever they bank, without payout minimums or losing revenue to payout fees."
---

If you're building a platform where creators, freelancers, or contributors get paid through tips, subscriptions, or revenue share, collecting payments from fans is only the first step. The harder challenge is getting those funds to creators who may be in another country, or using a financial institution your payout provider does not support.

Zelkea gives platforms two flexible ways to move money. Use the Payment API to split payments and settle funds instantly in a single transaction, or collect payments first and use the Transfer API to pay creators whenever you choose.

## Flow #1: Split payments in a single transaction

If you need a payment to be collected and instantly distributed between multiple parties, such as a creator and your platform, use the split payment capabilities of the Payment API.

```bash Platform fee + creator share, in one transaction
curl --request POST 'https://api.zelkea.com/payments' \
  --header 'Authorization: Bearer <API key>' \
  --header 'idempotency-key: 8e7f6a5b-4c7b-9e4b-3c2d-1e4f5a8b6c9d' \
  --header 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  --data-raw '{
    "customer": "fan22@bank",
    "amount": 10,
    "currency": "USD",
    "code": "P8L3Z1",
    "splits": [
      {
        "handle": "creator99@fr",
        "amount": 8.50
      },
      {
        "handle": "platform@us",
        "amount": 1.50
      }
    ]
  }'
```

Both you and the creator receive your share instantly. There is no need for a separate payout provider, and you never have to hold funds on behalf of your users.

## Flow #2: Collect a payment, then make a transfer

Alternatively, you can collect payments first and use the Transfer API to send funds to creators when needed.

```bash Collect a payment
curl --request POST 'https://api.zelkea.com/payments' \
  --header 'Authorization: Bearer <API key>' \
  --header 'idempotency-key: 8e7f6a5b-4c7b-9e4a-3c2d-1e4f5a8b6c9d' \
  --header 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  --data-raw '{
    "customer": "fan22@bank",
    "amount": 5,
    "currency": "USD",
    "code": "P8L3Z1"
  }'
```

Most platforms batch creator payouts weekly or monthly because sending money to individual creators, especially across borders, is expensive and slow through traditional payout rails. With Zelkea, paying a creator is simply a near-zero cost instant transfer to their handle:

```bash Initiate the transfer
curl --request POST 'https://api.zelkea.com/transfers' \
  --header 'Authorization: Bearer <API key>' \
  --header 'idempotency-key: 9e4a3c2d-1e4f-5a8b-6c9d-8e7f6a5b4c7d' \
  --header 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  --data-raw '{
    "sender": "platform@us",
    "receiver": "creator99@fr",
    "amount": 4.50,
    "currency": "USD"
  }'
```
